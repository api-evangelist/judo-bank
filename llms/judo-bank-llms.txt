# Judo Bank

> Judo Bank (Judo Capital Holdings Ltd) is an Australian challenger bank (ASX: JDO) purpose-built for SME lending and term deposits. As an accredited ADI and CDR data holder, it exposes a public, unauthenticated Consumer Data Right (CDR) Product Reference Data (PRD) API conforming to the DSB Consumer Data Standards Banking API. Authenticated consumer-data sharing is governed by the accredited-data-recipient (ADR) model, not an open developer program.

## APIs

- [Judo Bank CDR Product Reference Data API](https://www.judo.bank/open-banking/): Public unauthenticated product catalogue at https://public.open.judo.bank/cds-au/v1 - GET /banking/products and GET /banking/products/{productId}.
